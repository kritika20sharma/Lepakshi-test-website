"Lepakshi is not merely a temple — it is a living scripture in stone. Every pillar, every fresco, every corridor speaks of a civilisation that believed beauty itself was a form of devotion."

To the People of Lepakshi & All Who Journey Here,

It is with great pride and humility that I address you on behalf of the people of the Lepakshi constituency. Our land holds within it one of India's most exquisite examples of Vijayanagara architectural genius — the Veerabhadra Temple — a monument that has endured centuries and continues to inspire all who stand before it.

As your representative, I am deeply committed to the preservation, restoration, and promotion of Lepakshi's heritage. Our ancient craftsmen left us an incomparable inheritance: the hanging pillar, the monolithic Nandi, the intricate frescoes depicting stories from the epics — each a testament to the spiritual and artistic heights our ancestors reached.

This website has been created to share that legacy with the world — through architectural sketches, travel guidance, and the stories embedded in every stone. I encourage visitors, scholars, and pilgrims alike to come, see, and carry Lepakshi's story forward.

Let us together ensure that what was built with devotion is preserved with equal devotion.

Sri Nandamuri Balakrishna
