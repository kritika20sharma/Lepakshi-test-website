ProtoVillage

Spread across 25 acres of red-soil Andhra countryside, ProtoVillage is a living experiment in regenerative village life — mud-brick homes, organic kitchen gardens, and open-source knowledge sharing. Twelve kilometres from Lepakshi, it is the ideal complement to a day with ancient stone.

Government Heritage Dining · APTDC

Haritha Hotel, Lepakshi

Run by Andhra Pradesh Tourism, Haritha Hotel serves the definitive Telugu thali in a clean, no-frills dining hall — a fitting companion to a temple visit. The meal arrives in courses, unhurried, as it always has.
